5 Instructions to use the repository on your tablet or phone

To follow these steps you must have completed the instructions to create an Anki account and download the repository to your computer.

5.1 Open your App store (if you have an iPhone) or Play store (if you have an Android).

5.2 Look for the app called Anki.

The Anki icon looks like this:

5.3 Download the app.

Download the app on your tablet or phone. Please note that Anki is free on Google play, but requires payment on the App Store.

5.4 Link your account.

Enter the app and click on the three lines usually found on the left side of the screen.

5.5 Selecting these three lines will open the menu seen below.

Select where it says settings

5.6 Now select the “sync” option

5.7 Select the “AnkiWeb Account” option.

Enter your email and password.The same email and password you used to create the Anki account

Note: You may also be required to enter your Anki email and password as soon as you open the app.

5.8 It will take a few minutes to sync and then you will see the repository.
